Introducing the ...
Pegasus

The Pegasus will carry travelers around the Milky Way Galaxy in an extravagant way with many luxurious amenities ranging from Phat Duck entertainment to Potassium Palace. The whole entire trip will take one month and the passengers will get to the ship with the help of the Griffin. This ship is for travelers wanting to explore other planets and celestial bodies.

Specifications

Engines

Inside each of Pegasus’s six reactors is a cluster of eighteen Pegasus engines. These engines can run for a 100 years with only 10 kilograms of fuel. Since they are fusion reactors, they are very efficient and safe.

Capacity

Pegasus can hold up to 100,000 adults and 30,000 children. Fourteen or older is considered an adult and a child if they are younger.

Safety

Pegasus has 300 escape pods and each escape pod can hold 500 people. There are 40 more escape pods for extra safety. Multiple lightweight space suits will protect the travelers from the environment.

Gravity Generator

This will create artificial gravity that will make the traveling experience enjoyable.

Events

Outside

The revolutionary new game, space ball, will be played when the ship “parks” in space. The travelers will have a chance to win a free ride. Spacewalks will be held at predetermined times. The travelers will be given the most advanced spacesuit for protection. Travelers will have a chance to visit safe planets and they will be able to explore permitted areas.

Inside

There will be huge pools in the ship and the gravity generator will be turned off so the water would float. There are gyms, spas, classes and more interactive elements inside.

Price

You will have to call the office for an estimate. However, the ticket will cover the cost of Pegasus, Griffin, and anything bought in the Bruchak Space Center.
