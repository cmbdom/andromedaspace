Griffin

The Griffin will carry travelers to the Bruchak Space Center in an extravagant way with the food provided by Potassium Palace. The whole entire trip will take a day and the passengers will be able to go to the Pegasus (see the Price section for more information). This is for travels wanting to go to space and visit the Bruchak Space Center.

Specifications

Engines

Inside each of Griffinâ€™s three reactors is a cluster of nine Pegasus engines. These engines can run for a 1 year with only 10 kilograms of fuel. Since they are fusion reactors, they are very efficient and safe.

Capacity

Griffin can hold up to 10,000 adults and 3000 children. Fourteen or older is considered an adult and a child if they are younger.

Safety

Griffin has 30 escape pods and each escape pod can hold 500 people. There are four more escape pods for extra safety. Multiple lightweight space suits will protect the travelers from the environment.

Gravity Generator

This will create artificial gravity that will make the traveling experience enjoyable.

Events

There will be no events; the travelers will only stay in their designated rooms. Since this will only transport them to the Bruchak Space Center.

Price

You will have to call the office for an estimate. However, the ticket will only cover the cost of the Griffin and anything bought in the Bruchak Space Center will cost extra. The Pegasus ticket will be needed to ride Pegasus.
