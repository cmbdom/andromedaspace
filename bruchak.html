Bruchak Space Center

The Bruchak Space Center will be a hub either for travelers trying to ride Pegasus or for people trying to have a nice vacation in Earth’s orbit. The space station will be as big as a small city with many services that will make the traveling and relaxing period enjoyable. The ticket will cover the cost of the Griffin, rooms and many events held by the Center (see the price section for more details). This is for the travelers wanting to experience space in an enjoyable way, but not wanting to go far away from their home planet.

Specifications

Engines

Inside each of the center’s twenty reactors is a cluster of a hundred Pegasus engines. These engines can run for a 1000 years with only 100 kilograms of fuel. Since they are fusion reactors, they are very efficient and safe

Capacity

The Center can hold up to 500,000 adults and 200,000 children. Fourteen or older is considered an adult and a child if they are younger.

Safety

The Center has 1400 escape pods and each escape pod can hold 500 people. There are 200 more escape pods for extra safety. Multiple lightweight space suits will protect the travelers from the environment. There will be hospitals and treatment plants for the waste.

Gravity Generator

This will create artificial gravity that will make the traveling experience enjoyable.

Events

Outside

The revolutionary new game, space ball, will be played on the open courts of the station, but the traveler will stay in a protected shell. Spacewalks will be held at predetermined times. The travelers will be given the most advanced spacesuit for protection and highly trained professionals will lead them. People will be able to explore the surface of the moon in designated areas. The pods will transport them to the surface.

Inside

There will be huge pools in the ship and the gravity generator will be turned off so the water would float. There are gyms, spas, classes and more interactive elements inside. Travelers will have a chance to explore the experiments and labs of the most famous scientist and they can even try to learn the subject or object of study in our classes.

Price

You will have to call the office for an estimate. However, the ticket will cover the cost of Griffin, many of the events hosted in the Center and the rooms provided.
